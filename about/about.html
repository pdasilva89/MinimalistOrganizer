    Hello! My name is Suelene Souza. I decided to become a personal organizer because organization has always been a part of my life. Everywhere I go, in both personal and professional situations, I always end up helping people declutter their environments. My objective is to make the lives of my clients easier and more practical, so they have more time to do what they love. I work to understand my client's goals and needs and take that information to transform their living environments, utilizing tools that they already own.
